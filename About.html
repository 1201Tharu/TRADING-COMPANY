About us,

Since 1995, under the guidance of Kapila Kumara, our business has been a leader in delivering high-quality automotive solutions. With nearly three decades of experience, we specialize in the fabrication of hydraulic oil coolers and radiators for various types of vehicles, including JCBs, excavators, cranes, and tractors. Our expertise also extends to generator radiators, ensuring that your equipment stays cool and efficient.

In addition to our fabrication services, we provide a wide range of local and imported batteries, meeting the needs of a diverse market. We also offer wholesale supply of essential battery products such asbattery water, acid, and coolant. Our goal is to ensure that you have everything necessary to keep your vehicles running smoothly.

As part of our commitment to innovation, we produce state-of-the-art aluminum plastic radiators and heat exchangers. We also manufacture water heaters and other essential components for automotive and industrial use, helping to enhance performance and efficiency.

we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and service. Whether you need custom fabrications or reliable battery supplies, our team is here to deliver excellence every step of the way.
